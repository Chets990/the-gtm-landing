Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

The GTM ("we," "us," "our") is operated by Coldreachmedia. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the-gtm.com and our application, and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your name and email address, provided via Google Sign-In when you log in.
  • Product usage data: the website URLs you submit for analysis, the GTM strategies and prospect lists generated for your account, and your credit balance and usage history.
  • Payment information: when you purchase credits, payment is processed directly by our payment partner, Razorpay. We do not store your card, UPI, or bank details ourselves — we only receive confirmation that a payment was completed and for which plan.

How we use this information

  • To operate your account — track your credit balance, save your generated strategies, and let you resume previous sessions.
  • To process payments and apply purchased credits to your account.
  • To improve the product based on how it's used.
  • To contact you about your account or important product updates.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers needed to run The GTM, including our payment processor (Razorpay), our hosting provider (Railway), and data providers used to generate prospect lists (such as Hunter and Apollo) when you actively use those features.

Data retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active, so you can resume previous work. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us below.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by reaching out through our Contact page.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@the-gtm.com.
